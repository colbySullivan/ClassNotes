
Goal of Deep and Wide Lifecycle
	Maximize Quality, Resources, Predictability, and Flexibility to balance quality over quantity
	Minimize Risk and Waste / Rework

Stage -> Phase -> Milestone

Stages
	Pre-Production
		Concept Phase
			Pitch Milestone: High concept / Demo / Statement of Work / Pitches to "Greenlight" committees
			Prototype: Proof of Concept - Technology / Gameplay (POCT / POCG)
	Production
		Dev Phase
			Vertical Slice Milestone: 
				One complete "level" or section 
				Designated assets are defined as shippable 
				No more placeholders in levels 
				Significant portion of gameplay
			First Playable Milestone: 
				Entire game is playable from beginning to end without cheats
				All major features are functional and most minor
				Contains vertical slice level
				Placeholders are okay
			Alpha Milestone
				Feature complete
				All art and design are shippable
				Balancing and bug fixes might still remain
		QA Phase
			Beta Milestone:
				Open Beta
					Add required / suggested features to Alpha
				Closed Beta
			Release-Launch Milestone:
				Ready to submit
				Master build created and certified
				Archived
	Post-Production
		Marketing
		Maintenance
		DLC
